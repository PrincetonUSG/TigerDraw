FAQ

About TigerDraw
TigerDraw is a web application built by gcuenca, cscho, fge, and hannahh for COS 333. It is meant to make the process of finding rooms for room draw easier. To learn more about TigerDraw, please see 'Where Can I Learn More?' below.
Contact Us/Report A Bug
If you have found a bug or have any questions, comments, or concerns about TigerDraw, please reach out to us at tigerapps@princetonusg.com
Acknowledgements
Many thanks to Sata Sengupta, our team adviser, and Dr. Robert Dondero for their advice, mentorship, and support. We also thank our test users, who provided feedback during development.
Where Can I Learn More?
The TigerDraw team has written several documents that may be of help to users and future system administrators:

User's Guide: Our User's Guide is meant for any user of TigerDraw. It explains all the features on TigerDraw, along with tutorials about how to use the application.

Programmer's Guide: Our Programmer's Guide is meant for future system administrators of TigerDraw, or those who are interested in the tech behind TigerDraw. This document details our tech stack and the application's implementation.
There Are Rooms Missing on TigerDraw
It's true that not 100% of all available dorm rooms on campus are shown on TigerDraw. This is due to limitations on the data the team had access to during development. We apologize for the inconvenience. If you find that a particular room is not listed, please reach out to us at tigerapps@princetonusg.com and we will add it manually to our database.
