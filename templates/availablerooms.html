As of 3/30, TigerDraw's room list should be comprehensive, including rooms in New College West and New College East.
Additionally, room reviews from prior years have been added.
Finally, you can now open floor plan PDFs in the "Map" section by clicking on buildings and hovering above their respective floor number -- since First College will soon be razed, we are not including their floor plans, and we are still working on adding floor plans for the New Colleges.
(Special thanks to our newest TigerApps developers Adam Gamba '23 and Ben Chan '23.)

All groups and favorite rooms should have been saved before the update. If you run into problems, please reach out to us at tigerapps@princetonusg.com.

Past Room Draws

Rankings are based on previous draw times. Ex: if a room was picked by the first draw group, it will have a ranking of 1. If a college is chosen, we reorder rankings for rooms within the college from 1-last. If a college is not chosen, the ranking is taken from the overall room draw.
